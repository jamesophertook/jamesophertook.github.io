James is a Graphic Designer based out of Cape Cod, MA. Born a true Cape Codder but raised in Italy with plenty of additional traveling as a child, James has experienced very different cultures and is fluent in
both English and Italian.

Reflecting his personality, his style is very professional yet friendly and inviting. He brings an interactive and well-thought-out process with just
the perfect amount of detail and color (or lack thereof) to every project
he takes on.
